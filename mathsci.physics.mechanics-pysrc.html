 24   
 25      The acceleration of the mass is 
 26       
 27      a = L * d^2theta/dtheta^2 
 28       
 29      The force on the mass is 
 30       
 31      F = m * g 
 32       
 33      with tangential component m * g * sin(theta). 
 34   
 35      Thus by Newton's 2nd law, the angular acceleration is 
 36       
 37      m * L * d^2theta/dtheta^2 = - m * g * sin(theta) 
 38      d^2theta/dtheta^2 = - (g / L) * sin(theta) 
 39   
 40      To convert this to a first-order system, we define the angular velocity 
 41       
 42      omega = dtheta/dt 
 43       
 44      and rewrite the angular acceleration as 
 45       
 46      domega/dt = - (g / L) * sin(theta) 
 47   
 48      @type  t: number 
 49      @param t: current time

 53      @rtype: vector
